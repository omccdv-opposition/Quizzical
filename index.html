Instructions:

In this game you will be asked questions about the topic you just chose. If you answer a question correctly, you score a "mark" based on the difficulty of the question and how detailed/correct your answer was. These marks are converted to points.

After answering each question, you will be shown:

  1. the question
  2. your answer
  3. a "model" answer that would score a perfect mark
  4. the mark and points you scored
  5. a button that allows you to proceed.

You will also gain Spell Points as you proceed through the quiz. If you reach 100 Spell Points, they are converted to a Spell Card which can be used during a question to skip it and automatically score a perfect mark for all its parts. However, it is often better to save Spell Cards as you gain additional points from them at the end.

At the end of the game, you will be given a Level from 0 to 9 that reflects how well you did in the quiz. 9 is the highest level and 0 is failure.
